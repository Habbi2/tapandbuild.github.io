üìù Description

Short Description

Build your factory empire! Tap to earn coins, buy generators, prestige for multipliers, and compete on global leaderboards. Features offline earnings, daily challenges, and a unique grid placement system.

Long Description

Tap & Build is an addictive idle clicker game where you build and manage your own factory empire. Start by tapping to earn coins, then invest in 22 unique generators that produce income automatically.

Master the strategic grid system where placement matters - fill rows for bonus production. Hire 18 specialized managers to automate and boost your factories. When you've built up enough wealth, prestige to earn permanent multipliers and unlock powerful ascension perks.

For the ultimate challenge, transcend to the celestial realm where you'll earn Celestial Points and unlock an entirely new progression tree. Compete with friends in private rooms or climb the global leaderboards across 6 different categories.

Your factories keep producing even when you're offline - come back hours later and collect massive earnings!
