What is Tap & Build?

Tap & Build is a free idle factory tycoon game for Android. Tap to earn coins, buy 14 unique generators, prestige for multipliers, and compete on global leaderboards. Features include a live market with 3 investment indexes, managers, ascension perks, daily challenges, and offline earnings.

Is Tap & Build free to play?

Yes! Tap & Build is completely free to download and play. Optional in-app purchases are available to remove ads and get bonus features, but all game content is accessible for free.

Does Tap & Build work offline?

Yes! Your factories continue producing even when you're not playing. Come back after hours or days and collect all your offline earnings.

How do I prestige?

Once you've earned enough currency, you can prestige to reset your progress in exchange for prestige points. These provide permanent multipliers and unlock powerful ascension perks.
