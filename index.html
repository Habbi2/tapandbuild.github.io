Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tap & Build?

Tap & Build is a free idle factory tycoon game for Android. Tap to earn coins, buy generators that produce currency automatically, prestige for multipliers, and compete on global leaderboards. It features 14 unique generators, a Tetris-style factory grid, managers, ascension perks, daily challenges, and offline earnings.

Is Tap & Build free to play?

Yes! Tap & Build is completely free to download and play. Optional in-app purchases are available to remove ads and get bonus features like permanent 2x boost, but all game content is accessible without paying.

Does the game work offline?

Absolutely! Your factories continue producing even when you're not playing. When you return, you'll receive all your offline earnings. Hire managers to boost offline production up to 100%!

What makes this different from other idle games?

Tap & Build combines classic idle mechanics with unique features: a Tetris-style grid where you place and merge factories, 8 hireable managers with unique bonuses, ascension perks for deep progression, daily/weekly challenges, lucky drops, a lucky wheel, and competitive leaderboards.

How do I prestige?

Once you've earned enough currency, you can prestige to reset your progress in exchange for prestige points. These points provide permanent multipliers and can be spent on powerful ascension perks that persist forever.
