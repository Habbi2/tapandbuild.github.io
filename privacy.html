This Privacy Policy describes how Tap & Build ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Leaderboard Name: If you choose to submit your score to the global leaderboard, you provide a display name. This is optional and can be any name you choose.

Information collected automatically:

  • Game Progress: Your game data (currency, upgrades, achievements) is stored locally on your device.
  • Advertising Data: Our advertising partners (Google AdMob) may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the game experience
  • To display your score on the global leaderboard (if you opt-in)
  • To show advertisements through Google AdMob
  • To improve our game based on general usage patterns

3. Data Storage

Local Storage: All your game progress is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. We do not have access to this data.

Leaderboard Data: If you submit a score to the leaderboard, your display name and score are stored on our servers (Supabase) and are publicly visible on the leaderboard.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. Google's Privacy Policy
  • Supabase: For leaderboard functionality. Supabase Privacy Policy

5. Advertising

We display ads through Google AdMob to support the free version of our app. You can:

  • Choose to watch rewarded ads for in-game bonuses (optional)
  • Remove all ads through an in-app purchase

AdMob may use device identifiers and cookies to personalize ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Our game is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The only personal data collected is the optional leaderboard display name, which should not contain real personal information.

7. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data.
  • Leaderboard Data: Retained until you request deletion or the service is discontinued.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your leaderboard data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Clear your local game data at any time

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to request data deletion, contact us at:

Email: support@tapandbuild.com
